Forms - Capturing text

This example uses the val() function to capture the text entered into the form.

It's then displayed on the 'Submit' event in an alert box.

<form method="post">
  Enter any word:
  <br>
  <input type="text" id="entered_word">
  <br>
  <input type="submit">
</form>

$('form').on('submit', function(){
  //capture text that's entered into form
  var enteredWord = $('input#entered_word').val();
  alert(enteredWord);
});
